Events

Leather Keychain Workshop June 15, 2019 We invited Riley, the Vancouver-based leather artisan, to show participants how to make simple and cool leather keychains. Pre-cut leather strips, required materials and tools will be provided during the workshop. An easy DIY activity for big kids and their parents to enjoy some memorable time together for yours.

Art & Tea Evenings – Watercolour Painting Drop-In June 20, 2019 ‘Art & Tea Evenings’ We invite you to sit down with MUJI for a perfect ending to a long day, as we provide a place in-store to try out and practice the many different arts of the pen, brush, paper, and colour. This series aims to engage our community by bringing people together in a relaxing and meditative space to enjoy an evening of art and a warm cup of MUJI tea.
