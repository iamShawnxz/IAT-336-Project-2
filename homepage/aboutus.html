ABOUT US

HI is a Canadian grocery company which promoted various high-quality goods based on daily necessities such as clothing, daily groceries, and food. HI's potential customers was young people who love minimalism. The products focus on the concept of simplicity, environmental friendly and people-oriented. Product categories range from pencils, notebooks, and food to basic kitchen appliances.
